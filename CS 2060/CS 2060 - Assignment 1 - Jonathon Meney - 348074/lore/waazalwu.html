Waazalwu

The Waazalwu is a minor event occurring a few years after Departure. The Waazalwu begins with a violent earthquake. From this earthquake the 2 major islands, Ilora and Sithica, emerge from the sea. The Isles of Escalos also appear during this time.

These landmasses upon being discovered are also found to be inhabited already. New races also emerge. The island of Ilora contains two races known as:

  • Vedalken
  • Ilorian

The island of Sithica contains new races and subraces, known as:

  • Verdan
  • Deep Gnomes
  • Duergar

The Isles of Escalos also contains new races, known as:

  • Tortles
  • Yuan-ti
  • Locathah
  • Grung

The Waazalwu also causes slight mutations amongst humans across the realm. Theses mutations are especially prevalent amongst the people of Drin. This new mutated race becomes known as the Krin.
