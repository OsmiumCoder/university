Severance

Severance occurs shortly after the death of the final chromatic dragon at the end of the Discrepancy Wars. Severance first begins with a violent series of earthquakes as the land of Formatos changes into what is now known as Tuvern.

The blood of the dead dragons goes on to form the oceans that surround the 7 countries of Tuvern. In addition to the oceans forming the first Ley Lines appear giving Tuvern magic.

More races are born in the Cave of Emergence from the dragons blood. These races are known as:

  • Aarakocra
  • Dragonborn
  • Firbolgs
  • Genasi
  • Goliaths
  • Kobolds
  • Leonin
  • Lizardfolk
  • Loxodon
  • Sea Elves
  • Tabaxi
  • Tritons

The greatest age of humanity follows Severance known as the Age of Ideogony.
