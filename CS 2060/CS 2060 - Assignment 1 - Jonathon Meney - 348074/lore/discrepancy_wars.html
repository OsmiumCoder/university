Discrepancy Wars

The Discrepancy Wars begin when a group of nomads return to the Cave of Emergence where they find The Null Seal. The nomads, a group containing one of each of the races, manage to tamper with The Null Seal releasing chromatic dragons into the world. The nomads become known as The Discrepancy.

The actions of The Discrepancy begins the 30 year war between the races of humanity and the dragons. The chromatic dragons first began lighting settlements ablaze, and begin killing the Metallic dragons. The metallic dragons had been the protectors of humanity since the dawn of time and in turn humanity allied with them through the war.

By the end of the war almost all metallic dragons are killed and only 4 remain in the end after the last chromatic dragon is slain, and The Discrepancy is defeated. The 4 remaining metallic dragons become known as the The Great Wyrms, and include:

  • Edox, Watcher of the Cave
  • Algor, The Skywatcher
  • Alzon, The Silvered One
  • Edifix, Keeper of Brass.

The killing of the final chromatic dragon and The Discrepancy causes an event known as Severance.
