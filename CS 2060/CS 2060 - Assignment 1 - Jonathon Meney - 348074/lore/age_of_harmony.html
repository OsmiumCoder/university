Age of Harmony

The Age of Harmony is a short era of just 50 years. During this time hundreds of dragons roamed the lands as protectors. Only small settlements exist at this time and no major cities have been founded yet.

During this period, 3 more races develop, known as:

  • Half-Elves
  • Halflings
  • Gnomes

The Age of Harmony is concluded by the Discrepancy Wars.
