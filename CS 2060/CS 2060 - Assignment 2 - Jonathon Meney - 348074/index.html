Turtle Board Game/Drawer

Symbols

?: Unvisited location.
*: Visited location.
@: Turtle/Pen. Refer to controls below to move.

Controls

Use W, A, S, and D to move the Turtle.
W: Move up.
A: Move left.
S: Move down.
D: Move right.
Use P to raise or lower pen. The pen is up by default.

Board Size (default 50)
